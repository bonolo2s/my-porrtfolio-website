About Me


Passionately Crafting Code, One Bug at a time


As a self-taught programmer, my journey began at the University of Pretoria in 2020. Despite initial discouragement leading me to quit, I reclaimed my passion and taught myself how to program, demonstrating my resilience and determination.


I have a comprehensive understanding of the software development life cycle, with a particular interest in front-end development. Proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I’ve honed my skills through online courses, tutorials, and personal projects, building a portfolio that showcases my abilities.


My hardworking nature, resilience, and self-motivation fuel my love for challenges and learning new things. These qualities, combined with my passion for programming, make me a strong candidate for any front-end and email development role.


In my free time, I enjoy playing soccer. My goal is to continue learning and growing as a programmer, striving to create innovative and impactful web applications.
